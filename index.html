Starting small in Forest Hall.

Real jobs. Real support.

Free deep-clean “welcome home” packages for new parents — about 400 births a year in the Forest Hall postcode when fully scaled. 100% funded by the charity. Cleaners paid fairly. Software free to the programme.

Funding plan → Gift a clean

The offer

2 hours Deep clean package
£30 Cost per clean (£15/hr)
~400 Births / year (Forest Hall)

We’re starting hyper-local, proving it works, then scaling postcode by postcode — creating real jobs and real support for new families in the North East. This is the model discussed with Julie Marriott (Family Gateway).

Funding phases

All phases are 100% funded by the charity. Early phases are self-fundable personally; donations and gifts cover scale once public.

Extend or gift a clean

Parents can extend

Pay to extend any clean at £15 per hour (minimum 2 hours). 100% of every paid pound goes straight to the cleaner.

Relatives & friends can gift

Gift extra cleans the same way — perfect for baby showers or “welcome home” presents. Same rate; same rule: money to the cleaner.
