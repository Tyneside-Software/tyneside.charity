Welcome home.

Who benefits

New parents

A deep clean when you come home with a newborn — practical help when it’s needed most. No bill. No stigma. Just support.

Local cleaners

Paid, meaningful work through the programme. Not free labour — fair pay for professionals delivering community impact.

The region

Software runs the jobs in public. Capability stays local. Prosperity built by doing the work, not waiting for subsidies.

How a welcome-home package works

  1. 1

    Refer or request

    A family is identified locally — starting with our postcode area — and booked in for a deep clean.

  2. 2

    Cleaner dispatched

    A local professional is scheduled and routed through Tyneside Software.

  3. 3

    Home ready

    Parents return to a clean space. Cleaner is paid. Impact is real and measurable.
